# Preserve progress across Content Revisions

The target platform keeps Lesson identity stable while an explicit Content Revision marks a material change to its learner action or mental model. Existing Lesson Completion is preserved rather than reset, but the platform identifies Lessons revised since completion and invites the learner to revisit them; cosmetic edits, source refreshes, and Module moves do not increment the revision, while a fundamentally different capability requires a new Lesson slug. This balances honest update signaling with durable browser-local progress.
